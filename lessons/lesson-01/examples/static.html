World's Greatest Grilled Cheese

Recipe Ingredients

  • 2 tbl spns of butter
  • 2 slices of home baked bread
  • 2 slices of monteray jack cheese
  • 1 slice of old cheddar
  • 1 slice of mozzarella

Recipe Preparation Method

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  2. Place buttered side of each slice on griddle
  3. Place on piece of monteray jack on each piece of bread
  4. Place cheddar cheese on one slice of bread
  5. Place mozzarella on the opposite piece
  6. Once golden brown, combine both pieces
  7. Cut into triangles and serve with favourite your ketchup
